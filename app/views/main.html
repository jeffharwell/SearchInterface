HIT Description and Instructions

This HIT requires you to create a search that you think would help you answer a specific question that you will be given. You will be using a new type of search engine that constructs a search diagram using the terms that you type in.

Once you have completed the task you will be taken to a 9 question survey; 8 of the questions are muliple choice questions, one question is free form.

After you submit the survey you will be directed back to Amazon MTurk and your work will be automatically submitted

Please Note:

  • This task requires a recent version of Firefox or Chrome, please do not attempt this HIT with Internet Explorer or an older browser, it may not work.
  • Please carefully answer all 9 question on the survey to get credit for completing the HIT

Thank you for your consideration!
